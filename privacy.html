Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies on the collection, use and disclosure of your information when you use SuperBubble mobile game.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following non-personal device information automatically when you open our game:

  • Device model, operating system version
  • Advertising ID (AAID) for displaying in-game advertisements
  • Anonymous game play data, level progress for game optimization

We do NOT collect your name, phone number, email address or precise location.

2. How We Use Collected Data

  • Serve relevant advertisements via Google AdMob SDK
  • Fix bugs, optimize game performance and balance levels
  • Count anonymous game user statistics

3. Sharing Information With Third Parties

We share anonymous advertising ID with Google AdMob to deliver ads. All third parties are required to comply with Google Play privacy rules.

4. Data Storage & Security

All anonymous game data is stored securely and will not be sold or rented to any other companies for marketing purposes.

5. Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for general audiences aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13 years old.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via your Google Play Developer contact email.
