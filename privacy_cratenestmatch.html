Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application CrateNestMatch (hereinafter referred to as the "Game"), developed and published by HEBBAN FASHION TAILORING (hereinafter referred to as "we", "us", or "our").

1. Information We Collect

When you launch and play our Game, we may automatically collect the following information:

  • Device Information: Device model, Android OS version, device language, and network type.
  • Advertising Identifier (AAID): We collect your Android Advertising ID to serve relevant advertisements. You can reset or limit ad tracking in your device settings.
  • Gameplay Data: Anonymous gameplay records, level progress, and crash logs used strictly for game optimization.

We never collect your full name, phone number, email address, real-time precise geographic location, payment card information, or any other personally identifiable private data.

2. How We Use Collected Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Ad Delivery: We integrate third-party ad SDKs (such as Google AdMob) to display in-game advertisements. Your Advertising ID is used to deliver matched ads and measure ad campaign performance.
  • Game Optimization: To improve running performance, fix program crashes and bugs, and adjust game difficulty and content balance.
  • Analytics: To count anonymous global user volume, play retention, and gameplay behavior statistics, without linking this data to individual users.

3. Sharing Information With Third Parties

Because our Game is ad-supported, we share anonymous data with trusted partners:

  • Advertising Partners: We only share your anonymous Advertising ID (AAID) with Google AdMob and other integrated ad networks to serve advertisements. All third-party ad providers must fully comply with the Google Play Developer Program Policies and global data protection regulations.
  • No Data Sales: We will never sell, rent, or trade any user data to external marketing organizations.
  • Ad-Free Version: If you choose to purchase an ad-free version of the Game, all ad-related data collection and sharing will be immediately suspended.

4. Data Storage & Security

All anonymous game-related data is stored with industry-standard security protection. No personally identifiable information is stored on our remote cloud servers.

After you uninstall the Game from your device, all local cached gameplay data, advertising ID tags, and progress records will be permanently erased automatically.

5. Children's Privacy

All our games are intended for general audiences aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect or store any information from users under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and find your minor child has provided data to us, please contact us immediately, and we will fully delete all related records.

6. User Rights & Data Deletion Request

You have the right to request the full deletion of all anonymous game data associated with your device.

  • Send a deletion request email to Liguangliang@luckiads.com, with the email subject: Game Data Deletion Request (please add your specific game name in the subject for faster processing).
  • We will clear all cached anonymous device identifiers and gameplay statistics within 14 business days after receiving your valid request.
  • Since no personal private data is stored on our remote servers, uninstalling the game will fully remove all local data without residual retention.
