Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application WinterSkydiving (hereinafter referred to as the "Game"), developed and published by HEBBAN FASHION TAILORING (hereinafter referred to as "we", "us", or "our")..

1. Information We Collect

We may automatically collect the following non-personal device information when you launch our Game:

  • Device model, Android OS version, device language
  • Advertising ID (AAID) — only if the Game contains in-game advertisements
  • Anonymous gameplay records, level progress, crash logs for game optimization

We never collect your full name, phone number, email, real-time precise geographic location, payment card info or other personally identifiable private data.

2. How We Use Collected Data

  • If the Game integrates ad SDKs (Google AdMob): Deliver matched in-game advertisements to users.
  • Optimize game running performance, fix program crashes & bugs, adjust game difficulty and content balance.
  • Count anonymous global user volume, play retention and gameplay behavior statistics, without linking data to individual users.

3. Sharing Information With Third Parties

For ad-supported games: We only share anonymous Advertising ID with Google AdMob to serve advertisements. All third-party service providers must fully comply with Google Play Developer Program Policies and global data protection regulations.

For games without any advertisements: No user data will be shared with any third-party companies.

We will never sell, rent or trade any user data to external marketing organizations.

4. Data Storage & Security

All anonymous game-related data is stored with industry-standard security protection. No personal identifiable information is stored on remote cloud servers.

After you uninstall the Game from your device, all local cached gameplay data, advertising ID tags and progress records will be permanently erased automatically.

5. Children's Privacy

All our games are intended for general audiences aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect or store any information from users under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and find your minor child has provided data to us, contact us immediately and we will fully delete all related records.

6. User Rights & Data Deletion Request

You have the right to request full deletion of all anonymous game data associated with your device.

  • 1. Send a deletion request email to Liguangliang@luckiads.com, with the email subject: Game Data Deletion Request (you can add your specific game name in the subject for faster processing).
  • 2. We will clear all cached anonymous device identifiers and gameplay statistics within 14 business days after receiving your valid request.
  • 3. No personal private data is stored on our remote servers, so uninstalling the game will fully remove all local data without residual retention.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at: Email: Liguangliang@luckiads.com
